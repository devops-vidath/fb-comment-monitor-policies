Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

FB Comment Monitor collects and processes:

  • Facebook comments from pages you manage
  • Usernames and timestamps of commenters
  • Page IDs and post IDs
  • Your Facebook access token (encrypted and stored locally)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze comments for sentiment and offensive content
  • Generate reports and visualizations
  • Export data in CSV format for your use

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Store your Facebook credentials

4. Third-Party Services

This application uses:

  • Facebook Graph API - To read comments (read-only access)
  • Google Gemini API - To analyze comment sentiment

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted storage of access tokens
  • Local-only data storage
  • Read-only Facebook permissions

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all collected data at any time
  • Revoke Facebook access permissions
  • Export your data in CSV format

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date.
